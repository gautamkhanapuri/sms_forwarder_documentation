Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 10, 2026

Overview

SMS Forward is designed with your privacy as a top priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

ðŸ”’ Our Core Promise: We NEVER store your SMS message content on our servers. Messages are forwarded in real-time and immediately discarded.

Information We Collect

Data Type What We Collect Why We Need It
Authentication GitHub/Google profile (name, email, username) To create your account and authenticate requests
SMS Metadata Sender phone number, timestamp For logging and debugging (not message content)
Forwarding Rules Message patterns, email/Telegram destinations To know where and when to forward messages
Access Credentials Encrypted authentication tokens To securely authenticate API requests from your phone

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Contact Lists: We don't access your phone's contacts
  • Location Data: No GPS or location tracking
  • Device Information: No device fingerprinting or tracking
  • Browsing History: No tracking of your web activity

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Message Forwarding: Delivering SMS to your configured Telegram or email
  • Authentication: Verifying API requests from your mobile app
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing anonymous usage patterns to improve reliability
  • Debugging: Troubleshooting failed message deliveries (metadata only)

Data Storage & Security

Encryption

  • In Transit: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • At Rest: Passwords hashed with bcrypt (industry standard)
  • Tokens: Stored encrypted in database

Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Message Metadata (sender, timestamp) For analysis
User Account Data Until you delete your account
Forwarding Rules Until you delete them or your account

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Telegram API: For forwarding messages to Telegram (only if you configure Telegram forwarding)
  • Resend: For email delivery (only if you configure email forwarding)
  • DigitalOcean: Cloud server hosting with encryption at rest
  • GitHub/Google OAuth: For secure authentication (no passwords stored by us)

These services have their own privacy policies. We only share the minimum necessary data with them to provide the service.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your forwarding rules anytime in the mobile app
  • Modify: Edit or delete forwarding rules at any time
  • Export: Contact us for a copy of your data (metadata only)
  • Delete: Use the "Deactivate" button to permanently delete all your data
  • Regenerate Token: Get a new authentication token anytime

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data is only shared when:

  • You explicitly configure it (e.g., forwarding to your email means sharing with email provider)
  • Required by law (court orders, legal obligations)
  • Necessary to prevent fraud or security threats

Children's Privacy

SMS Forward is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
