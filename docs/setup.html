Setup Guide

Get started with SMS Forward in 5 simple steps

1

Download the Mobile App

Install SMS Forward from the Google Play Store on your Android device.

üì∏ Screenshot: Play Store listing
  1. Open Google Play Store on your Android phone
  2. Search for "SMS Forward by GAK Solutions"
  3. Tap "Install" and wait for download to complete
  4. Grant required permissions when prompted (SMS reading access)
üí° Tip: The app requires Android 8.0 (API 26) or higher. Make sure your device is updated to the latest Android version for best performance.
2

Login to Get Your Token

Visit the SMS Forward web portal and authenticate to generate your secure token.

  1. Go to https://gaks.solutions/sso
  2. Click "Login with GitHub" or "Login with Google"
  3. Authorize the application to access your basic profile information
  4. You'll be redirected back to the portal showing your profile
üì∏ Screenshot: Login page with GitHub and Google buttons
3

Register for SMS Forward Service

Once logged in, click the "Register" button to create your SMS Forward account.

üì∏ Screenshot: Profile page with Register button

After clicking Register:

  1. A unique authentication token will be generated for you
  2. The token will appear in a text box with a copy button
  3. Click "üìã Copy" to copy the token to your clipboard
Your token will look like this:

YWsleWrOirYjMMSE
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Keep your token secure! This token authenticates all forwarding requests from your phone. Don't share it with anyone. If compromised, use the "Regenerate Token" button to get a new one.
4

Configure the Mobile App

Open the SMS Forward app on your Android device and enter your token.

üì∏ Screenshot: Mobile app token input screen
  1. Open SMS Forward app on your phone
  2. Enter your biometric or pin
  3. On the configuration screen, click on the "Configure" button
  4. Paste your copied token in the password field
  5. Tap "Confirm"
  6. Tap the "Continue" button once it is enabled
5

Set Up Telegram (Optional)

If you want to forward messages to Telegram, complete this additional setup.

  1. Open Telegram and search for gaksmsbot
  2. Send the /start command to the bot by clicking on the Start button
  3. The bot will reply to you.
  4. In the SMS Forward mobile app, enter your Telegram username (e.g., @yourusername)
  5. Configure which messages should forward to Telegram
